Summer Salmon Swims

River of No Return | Exploring the Salmon

The Salmon River, nicknamed 'The River of No Return,' captured visitors' hearts with its rugged beauty, making many of them stay forever.

Flowing freely, the undammed Salmon River has higher, faster, and colder water in early summer and lower, slower, and warmer water in late summer. Although there are rapids throughout the season, families with swimming children often opt for the gentler water of late summer.

Desolate River Adventure

Discovering Desolation and Gray Canyon

Winding through canyons in central Utah, in some of the most desolate land anywhere, the Green river flows towards the mighty Colorado river and the sea.

Grey stone cliffs, desert landscapes, and rapids have made the 84-mile Sand Wash to Swaseys Rapid run famous. The trip offers stunning scenery, geologic formations, human history, and great whitewater.

Grand Canyon

Take Your New Aventure To The Grand Canyon

This is the grand daddy of all canyons and the grand daddy of all river trips. You will never forget your visit to the Grand Canyon.

Grand Canyon river rafting combines world-class whitewater with breathtaking scenery to make one truly unforgettable river experience. The canyon is filled not only with exhilarating whitewater rapids, but with side canyons and ancient indian ruins accessible only by river.
